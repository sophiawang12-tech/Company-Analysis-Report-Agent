# Analysis Configuration

## 1. Market Positioning
Description: Analyze the company's target market, core customer segments, and brand positioning. Focus on how it differentiates through marketing and product strategy.

## 2. Supply Chain Resilience
Description: Investigate the company's supply chain structure, key supplier relationships, and strategies for handling supply chain disruptions.

## 3. Digital Transformation
Description: Evaluate the company's investment in digital tools, cloud technology, and data analytics, with emphasis on AI and automation.

## 4. Financial Health
Description: Summarize recent financial performance including revenue growth, profitability, cash flow, and key investment areas.
